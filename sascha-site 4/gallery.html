The Three Environments That Formed Him

Bottmingen

The Swiss Castle Village · Basel

Where generational Swiss wealth quietly raises its next generation. Home to Switzerland's highest-income populations per capita. Schloss Bottmingen at its heart — a 13th-century moated water castle. Basel pharmaceutical heirs, private-bank partners, multi-generational Swiss-German industrial families.

◈ Champel Image Geneva · diplomatic quarter
46.20°N · 6.14°E · Age 15 — 34

Champel

Geneva's Diplomatic Quarter

The most refined residential district of Geneva — by any honest measure the global capital of diplomacy. Tree-lined avenues. Belle Époque buildings. UN senior officials, Ambassadors, WTO/WHO/UNHCR directors. Pictet and Lombard Odier private bank partners. Twenty years of native fluency in the register of global power.

◈ Crans-Montana Image Crans-sur-Sierre Golf Club · 1,500m
46.31°N · 7.48°E · Member Since Age 14

Crans-Montana

The Alpine Plateau of Legends

The Crans-sur-Sierre Golf Club — designed by Severiano Ballesteros, second course by Jack Nicklaus, home of the Omega European Masters since 1939. Every September: European aristocracy, Gulf royalty, discrete Swiss banking, quiet UHNW families. Sascha attended every year until leaving Switzerland.
